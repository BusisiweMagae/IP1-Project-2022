Daniel Githiomi

ALU, Course: Computing

Daniel is a competent, committed, self-motivated and adaptable software developer specializing in both Mobile and Web Applications Development. I am curious and enthusiastic about transferring my ideas into solutions for some of the most challenging technical problems of our generation with a belief that data-driven processes lead to great products and a diverse future. I desire to develop solutions to difficult problems, to create, innovate, and define the next era of modern software. With a logical perspective as well as a good eye for details, I am well conversant with both Back-end (Server-Side) and Fronted (UI/UX Design) programming.

I enjoy the challenge of learning new things in the ever-changing world of code and challenging myself to be better and improve on my skills. I find purpose in connecting people through the work that I do as a software developer in creating interactive software. I value integrity and loyalty, understanding that both are fundamental in building strong and lasting relationships be it professional or personal. Currently seeking an open opportunity within a progressive environment where I can put to use the knowledge I have accrued. I work exceptionally under no supervision, and I ensure that I deliver above-expected results in time. I'm strongly team-oriented and work well with others to make sure objectives are met and surpass any and all expectations.
